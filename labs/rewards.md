# Rewards Model

## Scoring
- Correctness: 40%
- Security: 25%
- Quality: 20%
- Collaboration: 15%

## Levels
- L1: Explorer
- L2: Builder
- L3: Guardian
- L4: Expert

## Rewards
- Points per Lab (50-500)
- Badges for milestone completion
- Bonus for clean SCA and well-documented evidence

## Anti-gaming
- Evidence must be reproducible.
- Hidden checks for Labs with rewards > 200 points.
- Manual review for anomalies.

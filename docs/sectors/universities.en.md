# Universities and Research

## Sovereign cognitive infrastructure for academia

ATLANTYQA reduces hyperscaler dependency and protects research data by keeping critical processing in local sovereign infrastructure. The model combines reproducible environments for teaching and AI with operational compliance (GDPR, DPIA, and ethics governance).

### What we deliver

- Local or hybrid infrastructure for research and teaching.
- Governed datasets and reproducible pipelines.
- Evidence-ready outputs for ethics committees and DPO review.
- Applied labs connected to real delivery environments.

### Why it matters

- Institutional control over strategic knowledge assets.
- Faster approval in ethics and data protection processes.
- Practical talent development aligned with real operations.

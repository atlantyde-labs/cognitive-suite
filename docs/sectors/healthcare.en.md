# Healthcare and Hospitals

- Modular pilots for lab, pharmacy, and medical reporting operations.

### Why it matters

- Better trust with medical leadership and data protection roles.
- Lower legal and ethical risk in AI adoption.
- Faster digital transformation with auditable evidence from day one.
